The Code Labs team has just returned from an incredible week in **Amsterdam** for **KubeCon + CloudNativeCon Europe 2026**! It was a week packed with technical insights, community building, and exploring one of Europe's most vibrant cities.

### 🇳🇱 Touchdown Amsterdam
Our journey began on Monday evening. After landing and checking into our hotel, we headed straight into the city center. There’s no better way to start a trip than with a long walk through the canals, getting a feel for the unique architecture and calm waterways of Amsterdam, followed by a solid burger dinner to fuel up for the week ahead.

![Amsterdam Canals](/assets/blogg/kubecon26/river.png)
*Cruising by the classic Amsterdam canals before the conference madness begins.*

![Arrival dinner](/assets/blogg/kubecon26/paer_beer.webp)
*Starting the trip with some local atmosphere and a well-deserved pint.*

### 🎟️ Day 1: Keynotes and First Impressions
Tuesday morning started early. We headed to the venue to pick up our badges and secure seats for the opening keynotes. The scale of the event was massive, and the energy in the room was palpable.

![The NVIDIA booth](/assets/blogg/kubecon26/nvidia_kubecon.webp)

### 🥨 Swag, Snacks, and 15,000 Steps
One thing you can always count on at KubeCon is the sheer amount of energy (and walking!) involved. We averaged around 15,000 steps per day navigating the huge halls.
