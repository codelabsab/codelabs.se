## What is Pickleball?

Pickleball started in 1965 in the USA and is a fun mix of tennis, badminton, and ping-pong played with paddles on a small court.
![pickleball court](/assets/blogg/pickleball-3.png)

## Pickla

Over the years of playing padel, I kept hearing about pickleball, and I really wanted to see why there was so much hype around it. So I took the team to Pickla in Solna/Sundbyberg. Our instructor, Gunnar (who owns the place), was very informative and even stepped in when one of us had to leave early. Big thanks to him!

Pickla [The Place](https://picklaparks.com/).
![pickla](/assets/blogg/pickleball-1.png)

Pickleball turned out to be awesome too!

![pickleball-padel](/assets/blogg/pickleball-2.png)

We played an Americano-style tournament, where everyone rotated partners and played 7-minute matches.

![pickleball-padel](/assets/blogg/pickleball-4.png)
