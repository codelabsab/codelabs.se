## Competitive Spirits and Taco Feasts: Code Labs by Kami

Our monthly **"Code Labs by —"** series is all about stepping away from the screens and strengthening our team bond through different activities. This time around, it was my turn to host, and I decided to take the crew to the underground playground known as **Ugglan Boule & Bar**.

### The Games: Speed, Spin, and Strategy

We kicked things off with a high-energy "2-kamp" featuring two of the most classic bar sports: **Ping-pong** and **Shuffleboard**.

![Intense ping-pong match with Pär and Tommy](/assets/blogg/codelabsby-pingpong-paer-tommy.webp)

The ping-pong table saw some serious action, especially when we switched to "rundpingis" (around the table), which quickly turned into a chaotic and hilarious test of reflexes. 

![Tommy focusing on the shuffleboard](/assets/blogg/codelabsby-tommy-shuffleboard.webp)

Over at the shuffleboard tables, the mood was a bit more calculated—though no less competitive—as we tried to out-slide each other for the top spot on the scoreboard.

![The shuffleboard court at Ugglan](/assets/blogg/codelabsby-shuffleboard.webp)

### Fueling the Competition

You can't have a Code Labs event without proper refreshments. Between matches, we enjoyed some downtime to catch our breath.

![Kami and Marre enjoying a beer](/assets/blogg/codelabsby-kami-marre-beer.webp)

* **Cold Drinks:** A few rounds of beer to keep the "athlete" nerves steady.
* **The Snack Platter:** A spread of olives, charcuterie, cheese, and almonds to keep us going.

![A delicious spread of snacks](/assets/blogg/codelabsby-snack.webp)

* **Taco Buffet:** To wrap up the night, we dived into Ugglan’s taco buffet, which was the perfect way to refuel and celebrate the winners.

### A Night to Remember

Events like these remind us that while we love solving complex problems during the day, we’re also pretty good at creating some friendly chaos at night. Thanks to everyone for the great energy!

![Final rounds of ping-pong with Pär](/assets/blogg/codelabsby-kami-paer-pingpong.webp)
