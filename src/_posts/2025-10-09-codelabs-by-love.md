## New Memories

I wanted to plan something fun for the team - something we hadn't done before. After a bit of searching (and rejecting a few questionable ideas), I stumbled on something unexpected... and a little bit dangerous. Perfect!

## Axe Throwing

Axe throwing!  
I found a spot in the city that offered this unusual but surprisingly fun experience.

After some quick safety instructions at [Max Axe](https://madaxe.se/), we were finally allowed to let loose and test our inner Vikings.  
![max axe throwing club](/assets/blogg/yxkastning_entre.jpg)

It turned out to be *way* more satisfying than expected. We learned a few different throwing techniques - the underhand throw was definitely the crowd favorite - and before long, things got competitive.  
![axe throwers](/assets/blogg/axe-throwers.jpeg)

## Dinner

After an hour of intense throwing and a few bullseyes, we walked over to [Yono Sabo](https://www.yonosabo.se/) for some well-earned tacos.

I love tacos - and these were honestly among the best I've ever had.  
![tacos](/assets/blogg/yono_sabo_tacos.jpeg)

We also went all in with a meat platter that didn't disappoint.  
![meat platter](/assets/blogg/yono_sabo_meat_platter.jpg)

All in all, it was a thrilling evening full of laughter, competition, and great food.
